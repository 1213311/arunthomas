Wiki Activity: Questionnaires

This is a review of the questionnaire used by Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) Security Services and FireEye Inc for their survey on “Cybersecurity Challenges, Risks, Trends, and Impacts”.

Review Summary:

The type of questions used for the survey were very generic and the options provided were not adequate enough to collect proper statistical data needed for the analysis. ?

Though initial few questions are focused on identification of the cyber security challenges organization face, the remaining questions challenged the overall objective of the survey itself as those are designed with the intention of identifying chances of selling commercial services to address the challenges. ?

The question used are more generic in nature and are not designed to specifically identify the People,Process and Technology related cyber security challenges organizations go through.

Survey Questions:

  • What are your organization’s top information-security challenges?
  • Across your organization, do you feel that you are experiencing more, fewer, or the same number of data attacks today compared with two years ago?
  • Where do you see the most growth in security threats?
  • On a scale of 1 (“inadequately prepared”) to 5 (“extremely well prepared”), how prepared is your organization to respond to an incident involving a material loss of information?
  • Does your organization have an information risk-management strategy?
  • How have security breaches impacted your organization in the past two years?
  • How prepared is your organization to detect each of the following kinds of security threats?
  • Does your organization currently have a plan for communicating with internal and external stakeholders following a security breach?
  • How do you rate your level of security in each of the following areas?
  • How prepared is your organization to prevent each of the following kinds of security threats?
  • How prepared is your organization to respond to each of the kinds of security threats?
  • Compared to a few years ago, do you feel that it’s easier or more difficult to do the following?
  • Does your organization have an information risk-management roadmap or tactical plan?
  • How frequently is your organization’s information risk-management roadmap or plan updated?
  • How often does your organization perform information risk assessments (that is, measure the inherent risk for the business or company)?
  • Do you track the long-term impact of security breaches in your organization?
  • If your organization has been breached, what changes to operations, compliance processes, and other activities did it make to help prevent and/or detectfuture breaches?
  • For each of these security- and risk-related functions, does your organization have the human resources and skills needed to adequately address them?
  • To what extent is your organization able to recruit, develop, and retain security talent?
  • For which of the following security- and risk-related functions does your organization currently use third-party service providers?
  • For which of the following security- and risk-related functions would your organization consider using third-party services?
  • Do you believe the management team or executives at the highest level of your organization truly understand the time and resources needed to mitigate risk and minimize cyberthreat exposure?
  • Do you benchmark your security expenditures?
  • What percentage of your IT budget is earmarked for cybersecurity?
